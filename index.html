Discover Sulawesi

This is story of two lands, told trough nature and culture. Of Manado, with its endless blu seas, majestic volcanoes, and a vibrant spirit shining through every smile. And of Gorontalo, with its serene lakes reflecting the sky, traditions preserved across generations, and a warmth deeply rooted in its people.

Two regions, two souls, one meaningful journey. This is a story of flavors, colors, and sounds a story that has pulsed through centuries and is now ready to open its most breathtaking chapter.

We are the travel company that breaks through the surface of typical tourism to take you deep into the real stories of Manado and Gorontalo. Wander through lively markets, visit heritage-rich villages, dive into mesmerizing coral reefs, and watch sunsets that blanket both lakes and oceans. Above all, meet the extraordinary people who will make this journey a part of your own story.



Join us and enjoy exclusive, authentic travel experiences that bring you closer to the heart of Sulawesi

Our Teams

Mr Daniel

Daniel is the Founder and Business Development Manager of GoroAdventure, in his pursuit for all things outdoors and to take life by the horns (Carpe Diem!), he has set up this diving base in Gorontalo as both the place and Marine life is relatively untouched, giving depth to the meaning of tranquil days, siesta afternoons as you awaken your senses to newfound freedom underwater. Come join Dan and his bros to scale your next mountain and conquer your Blue Ocean!

Mr Daniel

Mr Noldi

Noldi is a Master Dive with more than 25 years of diving experience under his belt, he has made diving both fun and safe for many people, who have been under his charge. Friendly, experienced and helpful are common words used to describe him. Come explore the Underwater World in Gorontalo with him. He is also a very accomplished Chef! Make sure to greet him and be in his good books, who knows... He might just treat you to an array of seafood, fresh from the sea!

Mr Noldi

Mr Yunan

With more than 15 years of all-rounded experience, Unan is known amongst his peers and guests alike to be zealous, knowledgeable and passionate towards Adventures spanning from Gorontalo and beyond. He has a wide network of friends and is a strong and agile Operations planner. You are in good hands under his care. Look for him if you wish to experience local culture, food, snacks, drinks and more!

Mr Yunan
