Review

Giles Tay

★★★★★

We had an Awesome time at Goroadventures. Great food, comfortable stay, friendly and staff, and a great host. One if my best trip so far.

Karyn Wong

★★★★★

Came here for a snorkeling/diving trip and my itinerary included snorkeling with the whale sharks and diving. Room is big, clean, toilet is great too. I love that they use rough surface flooring, so it’s safer for us in the shower. The sea view was also amazing. Service here is wonderful, with friendly staff. They also have a karaoke system, and can set up a jacuzzi for you to enjoy. The local fare prepared by the resort staff was delicious. I especially enjoyed the barbecue night where we had grilled fish. The pick up from the airport was also smooth. We felt really safe, and the entire trip from the airport to the resort was very enjoyable. If you are a diver, the underwater seascape here is BEAUTIFUL. If you are a coral lover, you’ll definitely love the dive site named Bosel. It was my first time seeing Salvador Dali Coral, and a huge one in fact. Thank you Noldi for showing us around!

Charlize Arundell

★★★★★

Noldi is very knowledgeable, speaks great English and is a great dive guide. He helped us with everything we needed. The location of the place is ideal for whale shark point and a supermarket across the road. I would recommend this place for anyone coming to Gorontalo to stay here!

Alexander Glock

★★★★★

Perfect place to spend one or two nights in Gorontalo. Nice new bungalows with a view to the whale shark point. The brothers Yunon and Noldi and the team are great hosts, always friendly and helpful. Great fresh food, we felt very welcomed. Thank you! Yunon took us on a tour through and around Gorontalo and organized the ferry tickets to the Togian islands. Best to contact Yunon via WhatsApp.

Cecilia Donati

★★★★★

We had a very nice time here! The place is brand new, big bed and big toilet! We also organised dive and the reef is very interesting with a lot of soft coral and sponge!

Chris Pallmer

★★★★★

With pak Noldy, they have the best dive guide of the area in their team. He will spot any animal underwater, no matter what size. Their equipment is brand new and state-of-the-art and the location is literally next to where the whale sharks hang out! What a pity their mahogany cottages were not yet ready to stay in – then it will be even better. I will definitely come back. Thanks Dan and Noldy.
