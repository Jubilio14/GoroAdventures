About Your Trip

Gorontalo is a hidden gem in northern Sulawesi, offering world class dive sites, magical encounters with whale sharks, a rich coastal culture, and authentic local cuisine. From exploring the vibrant coral gardens of Olele Marine Park to immersing yourself in time-honored village traditions, this journey invites you to reconnect with nature, slow down, and feel the warmth of Gorontalo’s heartfelt hospitality a tropical escape you’ll never forget.
